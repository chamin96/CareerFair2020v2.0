Are you Looking for Young Energetic Engineers?

Ready to open opportunities of the best industry positions to UOP products?

Ready to pick the best contenders for your esteemed organization?

Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya is ready to hold an online Career Fair in this year to enable this interface for you to pick the best contenders for your esteemed organization and simultaneously to open opportunities of the best industry positions to our products.

In contrary to the one-day Career Fair, this online Career Fair will spread out during two weeks starting from August 03, 2020. This will have an ample choice for you to select the most convenient times to have dialogues with our graduates and will save your time in travelling.

Our undergraduates are specialized in Chemical and Process Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Engineering, Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Manufacturing & Industrial Engineering. We will be sending you a list of candidates prior to the interviews so that you could have the opportunity to select the cream for discussions. We also will be extending our service by arranging zoom slots on your requst to have this dialogue according to your convenience.

Starting from August 03, 2020
